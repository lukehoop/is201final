Our Story

Woof woof woof woof woof. Woof-woof woof woof (woof woof) woof; woof woof. Woof woof woof woof woof woof woof.

  • Our Founder's Vision

    After being born at a very young age, Goldie immediately had big dreams, and knew she was destined for greatness.

  • September 17, 2008

    Goldie's ideas come to life

    As the 12 time Olympic nose balancing champ, Goldie has never encountered a challenge she couldn't face (or balance on her face). Her devotion to grilled cheese in unmatched on this side of the Mississippi. Her kindness to animals cannot be overstated, after keeping a live bird alive inside her mouth for an extended period of time. She enjoys long romantic walks by the pool and wants someone adventurous who gives her peanut butter jars.

  • June 20, 2017

    Nala steps onto the scene

    Amassing worldwide fame as the well-known inventor of the Nonut™, professional scuba diver, and semi-pro squealer (pre-career-ending injury), Nala always brings excitement and passion to the table (and eats the leftovers from it too).

  • October 30, 2022

    Da Ruth joins the team

    Renowned for her ability to eat non-edible items (including a turkey carcass, entire Easter basket of chocolate, etc.), and her incredibly deep voice, Da Ruth has been an invaluable addition to our team.
